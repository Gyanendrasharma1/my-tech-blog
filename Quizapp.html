Welcome to the Quiz App! Test your knowledge and challenge your thinking skills with our fun and interactive questions. Here's how you can play:

  1. Carefully read each question displayed on your screen.
  2. If you’d like to start over, simply click the 'Reset' button to clear all your selections and try again.
